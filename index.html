Why he is the best?

He has the most number of records in football history. Most number of individual awards. He was the best, He is the best, He will be the best.

Most balon d'or

In the history of football he is the only player to win 7 balon d'or. He won his first in 2009 and he also holds the record for most consecutive balon d'or won. He had a streak of 4 years. And one in 2015, 2019 and 2021. It is almost impossible for any player to break his record.

Most golden boot

The European Golden Shoe, also known as European Golden Boot, is an award that is presented each season to the leading goalscorer in league matches from the top division of a European national league. The trophy is a sculpture of a football boot. Messi has 6 Golden boots.

Highest goal ratio

Messi has the highest goal ratio. It is the ratio of number of goals to the number of games played. It defines the pure goalscoring ability of the player. He has a ratio of 0.85 and he has played more than 900 games in his career. It means that he score 85 goals in 100 games or 0.85 goals per game.
